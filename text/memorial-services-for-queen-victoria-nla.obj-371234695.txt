Prime Minister’s Office,
Sydney, 23rd January, 1901.
|~T is with extreme sorrow that His Excellency the Governor-General of the Common-
wealth of Australia directs it to be notified, for general information, that he has
received the melancholy intelligence of the demise of Her late Most Gracious Majesty
QUEEN VICTORIA.
I
Her Majesty expired at her Palace at Osborne, on the evening of the 22nd instant,
at 6'30 p.m., to the great affliction of the Royal Family and of all classes of Her Majesty’s
subjects.
By His Excellency’s Command,
EDMUND BARTON.
