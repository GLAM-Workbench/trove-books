INTRODUCTION.
The man in the street has always shown
a live interest in the subject of domestic architecture
but during these post-war years, this interest has
increased to such an extent that it has been found
impossible to'satisfy his need for literature on the
subject. The Sydney Municipal Library has tried to
buy as many books as its funds will pemit, dealing
with house construction, home design, gardening, home
repairs and domestic science and it offers this book-
list as a guide to these purchases.
Undoubtedly many good books bn these subjects
will not' have' received mention in the List* Indeed,
certain works which are closely related to the general
subject field of the List such as books on Strength of
Materials, Strains and Stresses, Steel Construction,
Sewerage, Water Supply, Fertilizers, Sicils, Fruitgrowing,
Child Care and Dressmaking have been purposely omitted
from the' list, because it was felt that they were not
exclusively associated, with house design and construction
and the making of a home•
It is not suggested that this classified
index to, our collection of books -oh the house and the
home will help to solve the immediate problem of
providing adequate and pleasing shelter for the house-
starved people of Sydney. However, it is believed
that an intelligent use of this guide'to the literature
made freely available to the citizen by the Municipal
Council of Sydney, will, help him to acquire ah expert
knowledge of house design and thereby permit him to
express an informed opinion on one of’the most important
subjects affecting his private and social life..
