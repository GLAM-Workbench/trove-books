ORDER OF BUSINESS
1. NATIONAL ANTHEM:
2. PRAYER:
Almighty God, we humbly beseech Thee to bless this Council. Make us worthy servants of
Thine and of Her Most Gracious Majesty, Queen Elizabeth the Second. Direct and prosper
our deliberations to the advancement of Thy glory and the true welfare of the people whom
we serve. This we earnestly ask through Jesus Christ our Lord. Amen.
3. WELCOME TO VISITORS BY SHIRE PRESIDENT: Cr. J. P. Mason, J.P.
4. INTRODUCTORY REMARKS BY SHIRE PRESIDENT:
5. CONSIDERATION OF THE FOLLOWING MOTIONS:
MOTION OF THANKSGIVING AND LOYALTY
Cr. D. W. Clark, J.P. will move
“That this meeting of the Council of the Shire of Bet Bet assembled to commemorate the Cen-
tenary of Local Government in this Shire express heartfelt gratitude to Almighty God under
whose benificent guidance, this Council and its residents has prospered and progressed during
the past century and conveys on behalf of the President, Councillors and Ratepayers of the
Shire, to His Excellency the Governor of the State of Victoria an assurance of fealty, devotion
and attachment to the Throne and Person of Her Most Gracious Majesty Queen Elizabeth the
Second."
Cr. J. J. A. Freemantle, J.P., to second
MOTION OF APPRECIATION
Cr. A. Brownbill, J.P., will move
“That this Council now records its sincere appreciation of the efforts of all former Presidents,
Councillors and Ratepayers of this Shire, who have by their united efforts brought this muni-
cipality to its present state of prosperity and progress.”
Cr. K. Jardine, J.P., to second
MOTION OF CONTINUED ENDEAVOUR
Cr. A. G. Gordon will move
“That this Council now pledges itself to continue at all times to further enhance the works al-
ready carried out to improve the conditions, amenities and welfare of the residents of this
municipality."
Cr. S. J. Bryant to second
6. ELECTION OF CHAIRMAN OF COUNCIL for year 1964 - 65:
The Hon. the Minister of Local Government, Mr. Rupert J. Hamer, M.L.C., will instal Chairman
and address meeting.
7. MOTION OF APPRECIATION OF ATTENDANCE OF MINISTER OF LOCAL GOVERNMENT:
Cr. R. A. Gray, J.P., will move
“That the meeting of the Council of the Shire of Bet Bet records its sincere appreciation for
the attendance of the Honorable the Minister of Local Government and other visitors at this
Centenary meeting of the aforesaid Council.”
Cr. J. P. Mason, J.P., to second
Responses.
