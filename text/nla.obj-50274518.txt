Total number of Australian plant-genera, recorded hitherto, 2,249.
The indications of the places, at which these additional genera
should be inserted into the systematic census, are not intended to
convey always an enunciation of close affinity to proceeding or
following genera, as often those, nearest allied, are quite wanting
in the Australian vegetation, or have as yet not been discovered
in this part of the world. The genera of evasculares have been
respectively determined by illustrious specialists, namely by
Gottache, Stephani, J. Mueller, Knight, Cooke, Saccardo, J. Agardh,
Kuetzing and Nordstedt. For the many additional genera of
minutest fungs Mr. F. M. Bailey’s collections, elaborated by
Professor Saccardo, afforded largely the material on this occasion.
