Maranai lesu i tuputupua au Beteleem,
mai ludea ai melagai, Eroda i viviwavalatona
lana, babada girugirumai au mara matana i
vomairi ma au Jerusalem i nei, i pa, Mai ludea
.ai Wavalatona i tuputupuiana mepa ? tauai
tauna ana übona au mara matana a inanai ma
weka a getai ma tauna awarina ana raudune.
Ma Eroda nuanuana i bolai ma ana rava
anatapui. Ma piriti i vibagai Keriso mepa i
ta tupua. Ma i riwa i pa, Au Beteleem;
nanare peroveta i riwa. Ma Eroda mai babada
i vibagai übona melanai i ega. Ma au Bete-
leem i paritawanei, i pa, ona nae, ona baiei;
ma o na nonori, ma ona riweu. Anina ma i
nae, ma übona au naoi i nae ma i vomairi au
melagai tevera e mamae lana. Ma i inanai, ma
nuanuai i ai, ma au numa i rui, ma tevera, ma
tauna alona Maria i inanai, ma i raudune, ma
ai pulo i verei. Ma leova i vigaei, ma ega
Eroda awarina ita ne memei, ma ai au dobu i
nae me.
Anina ma anela losepa i riwei i pa, Ma
vomairi, ma tevera ma alona au Aigipita ma
naiei; Eroda viragenana e goegoei. Ma i nae, ma
au Aigipita i mae. Ma Eroda i notai babada i
geduaiei, ma tauna i gaiawa, ma i paritawanei,
ma roaroa orotoi anatapui au Beteleem ai ma-
dega ruaga i virageni. Ma Eroda i irage, ma
anela losepa awarina i nei i pa, Ma vomairi,
ma am au dodu ma nae me ; tevera ana gavia i
irage pai. Ma i nae, ma mai Iseraela ai au

THINGS I MUST NOT DO.
1. I must not kill any person.
2. I must not hurt any person.
3. I must not threaten to hurt any person.
4. I must not steal.
5. I must not hurt anything that belongs to
another person.
6. I must not commit adultery.
7. I must not tell lies about the Government
8. I must not tell lies about other persons.
9. I must not try to stop the policeman
making fast any person.
10. If the policemen want to make me fast
I must not fight them.
11. I must not hurt cocoanut trees.
12. I must not kill any birds which the
Government say must not be killed.
13. I must not cut down or hurt trees which
the Government say must not be cut
down or hurt.
14. I must not spoil any roads.


THINGS I MUST DO.
1. When I speak as a witness I must speak
the truth.
2. I must bury dead people in the cemetery.
3. I must help to make roads and to keep
them in repair.
4. I must obey the Governor and the Magis-
trates, and the Government chiefs, and
the policemen.
5. If I agree to work for any person, I must
work for that person for the time I
agreed to work.
