NOTE
Labyrinth Press was established in 1980
by Thierry Bouchard and Petr Herel. The typography
was exclusively typeset and printed on letterpress by
Thierry Bouchard in Saint-Jea France.,
with the exception of book No. XVIII,
printed by Danie Mellor and book No. XXI,
printed by Caren Florance in the
Graphic Investigation Workshop,
in Canberra, Australia.
Etchings and graphic work were
by Petr Herel, with the exception of book
No. XVI, completed in collaboration with the
Swiss artist Jean-Eduard
From 1980 until2oo7, twenty-one titles were
published. Their poetic content and editorial philosophy
were based on the co-founder's affinities and were often
published in multilingual original translations.
The Labyrinth Press ceased activity in 2008
with the passing of Thierry Bouchard.
Petr Here!,
April 2013
