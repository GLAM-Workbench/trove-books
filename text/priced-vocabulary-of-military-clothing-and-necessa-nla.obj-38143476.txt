PRICED VOCABULARY OF MILITARY CLOTHING
AND NECESSARIES.
Published in accordance with AM. Reg. 1093.
1. The rates quoted in this edition of the “ Priced
Vocabulary of Military Clothing and Necessaries ” will
govern the charges for all issues on payment, unless
otherwise directed, and will be the basis for charges for
articles lost, damaged or destroyed by neglect, during the
period Ist July, 1921, to 30th June, 1922.
2. The prices for clothing materials (Section 7) are not
intended to govern sales to Government Contractors, as
special rates are arranged in these cases, but sales to
private firms for use on other than Government orders
will be made at the prices shown herein, plus 2| per cent.
3. The designations in the first column have been made
as short and distinctive as possible, and will generally be
sufficient for use in ledger headings, requisitions, vouchers,
and returns, but if in any particular instance this is not
so, additional information should be added to identify the
service for which the article is required. The sectional
arrangement of the articles must also be adhered to when
requisitioning stores on the Ordnance Branch.
4. Each article has in addition been assigned a “ Cata-
logue Number.” This number will be quoted on all
packages taken from stock for transit by boat or rail in
lieu of markings showing nomenclature. The quantity,
sizes of contents, and, when necessary, the number of bale
will be shown. For example, bale No. 20, containing 50
Service Dress Jackets, size 10, will be marked thus —
5. Any amendments found necessary will be published
in Military Orders from time to time.
6. Articles which are not of current manufacture, the
existing stock of which will not be replaced, are shown in
italics.
