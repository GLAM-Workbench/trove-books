

1854
VICTORIA.
MARTIAL LAW INDEMNITY BILL.
Ordered by the Council to he printed, 14 th December , 1854
CHAS. BOTH AM, Message No. 32.
Lieutenant Governor.
In pursuance of the provisions of the Statute 5 and 6 Vic., c. 76, the Lieutenant
Governor transmits to the Legislative Council, for its consideration, the draft of
? A Dill to indemnify all persons who have acted under the authority of Martial
? Law within a certain district in Victoria.?
Government Offices ,
Melbourne , 14 th December , 1854.
B.?No. 32
By Authority: John Ferres, Government Printer, Melbourne.
