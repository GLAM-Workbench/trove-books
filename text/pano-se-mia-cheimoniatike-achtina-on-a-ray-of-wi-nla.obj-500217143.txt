στις φαρδιές ωμοπλάτες του υπνου
άκόμη κι 5 δταν σέ ποντίζουν

Years ago, you told me :
“ In essence, my substance is light ”
And now, again, whenever you rest
on the broad shoulders of sleep,
or even when someone casts you
to the narcotised bosom of the sea,
you’re seeking in lost corners where blackness
has worn away and no more resists,
groping, you search
for the spear destined to lance your heart,
that it may open to the light.
