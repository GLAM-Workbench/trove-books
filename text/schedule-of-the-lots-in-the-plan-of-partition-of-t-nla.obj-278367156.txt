nUm That the property shall be divided into 11,248 lots, according to

That each ticket shall represent one of the lots contained in the said 1
“ Schedule of lots” hereunto annexed.
That the mode of ascertaining which lot each ticket represents or
shall represent as aforesaid, shall be by drawing by lot as follows, —that
is to say: —
That the drawing shall take place by means of two wheels, by the
hands of disinterested persons to be selected for that purpose.
That into the one wheel (to be called the “ Ticket Wheel”) shall be
put numbers printed in red ink on separate cards, representing the first
numbers on each folio of the Register of the Drawing, viz.. Nos. 1, 51,
101, and so on.
That into the other wheel (to be called the “ Lot Wheel”) shall
be put numbers printed in blue ink on separate cards, from No. 1 to
No. 11,248 inclusive, representing respectively the several lots comprised
in the said annexed “ Schedule of Lots.”
That there shall first be drawn from the “ ticket wheel” one ticket
number which shall be entitled to the first drawing from the “lot wheel.”
The Register being opened at that ticket number, one lot number shall
be drawn from the “ lot wheel,” which shall be entered in the said
Register opposite to the said ticket number, and the said ticket number
shall thereafter represent and be entitled to the lot or property so drawn
and entered against it. <
That one lot number shall then be drawn from the “ lot wheel” for
each of the ticket numbers on the same folio consecutively, and entered
in the said Register opposite the same respectively, which said ticket
numbers respectively shall thereafter represent the several lots or proper-
ties so drawn and entered against them respectively.
That there shall then be drawn from the ticket wheel another ticket
number which shall be entitled to the next drawing from the “ lot
wheel,” which ticket number and the following ticket numbers in the
same folio shall be drawn for and entered in the register as aforesaid, and
so on until the ticket numbers are exhausted and the drawing completed.
That such drawing shall take place, commencing on the first day of
January next, at the City Theatre, at such hour as shall be intimated.
