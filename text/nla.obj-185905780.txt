The amount raisod on behalf of the Wesleyan Church
Extension Fund is devoted to the support of those
Wesleyan Ministers who are labouring in the small
and thinly populated parts of Tasmania, where the
voluntary contributions of the people are not suffici-
ent for paying the stipend of the Ministers, and other-
wise sustaining the interests of the work in which
they are engaged. The distribution of the Fund
takes place at the Financial District Meeting, which
consists of a Committee of Ministers and lay gentle-
men annually appointed for the purpose. The follow-
