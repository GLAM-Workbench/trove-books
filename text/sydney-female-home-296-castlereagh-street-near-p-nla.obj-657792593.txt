

SYDNEY
Female Some,
296, CASTLEREAGH STREET,
NEAR PARK STRET.
THIS INSTITUTION has been formed to
provide Servants and Needlewomen, of Good
CHARRACTER, with a comfortable and respectable
Home, when out of place; such persons are
supplied with Board and Lodging for the sum
of 9s. per week, with the privilege of obtaining
Situations through the Society’s Registry
Office.
All Servants, whether out of place or not,
can become Members of the “Home,” upon
payment of 5s. annually.
For further Particulars application may be
made to the Matron, at the Sydney Female
Home.
Reading and Wellbank, Printers.
