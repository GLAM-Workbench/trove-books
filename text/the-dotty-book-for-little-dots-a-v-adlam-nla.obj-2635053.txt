INSTRUCTIONS
THE pictures in this book are incomplete. To complete
them take a sharp pencil and draw a straight line from
dot number 1 to dot number 2, then from dot number 2 to
dot number 3, and so on, until the pidture is completed.
CONNECT THE DOTS, NOT THE NUMBERS. Look carefully
for the next dot before starting to draw. The pictures are
very easily coloured with paints or crayons, and little artists
should try their skill in finishing each page in colour.
