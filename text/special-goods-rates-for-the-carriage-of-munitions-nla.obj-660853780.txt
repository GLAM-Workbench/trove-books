SPECIAL GOODS RATES FOR MUNITIONS AND
SPECIFIED DEFENCE MATERIALS.
Commencing 17th February, 1941, and continuing for the duration of the
war, the following Special Defence Rates and Conditions shall apply for the
carriage of the consignments specified in Clause 4 :
1. SPECIAL DEFENCE MILEAGE RATES.
(a) New South Wales Intebsystem— per ton per mile.
Intbasystem (Local) —-Special Defence Mileage Rates
provided in Clause 5.
( b ) South Australia.. Intebsystem—2d. per ton per mile. For traffic to or
from metropolitan stations, ordinary mileage rates
or the special rates in Clause 6 shall be added to the
Intersystem rate of 2d. per ton per mile between
Mile End and the border station.
Intbasystem (Local) —Special Defence Mileage Rates
provided in Clause 5, except that rates in Clause 6
and certain agreed rates in South Australia shall
be maintained.
(c) Victoria .. Intebsystem ob Intbasystem (Local) —Special
Defence Mileage Rates provided in Clause 5.
The rates on traffic forwarded via Albury shall be
computed on the actual mileage to or from that
station, which is 191 miles from Melbourne, 469
miles from Serviceton, for the Victorian proportion,
from which terminal charges shall not be deducted.
(d) Commonwealth .. 2d. per ton per mile between Eastern States and
Western Australia only.
(e) The freight rates specified above cover haulage only and do not include
incidental service charges such as loading, unloading, &c.
(/) Where existing rates are cheaper, they shall continue to apply.
2. JOURNEYS APPLICABLE TO.
(i) Intebsystem Tbafeic. —The rates shown in Clause 1 for Intersystem
traffic shall apply to the traffic shown in Clause 4 forwarded on
Intersystem journeys over the railway systems of two or more of the
following States, i.e., New South Wales, Victoria, South Australia
and the Commonwealth, the latter so far as traffic between the
Eastern States and Western Australia only is concerned.
(ii) Intbasystem (Local) Tbafpic.—The rates shown in Clause 5 shall
apply to the traffic shown in Clause 4 forwarded on local journeys on
the New South Wales, Victorian or South Australian Railways (see
Clause 1 ( b ) ).
Exceptions to (i) and (ii). —The Special Defence Mileage Rates provided for
Intersystem or Intrasystem traffic shall not apply to any local journey or to any
portion of an Intersystem journey of less than 50 miles on any system for which
the ordinary rate or such special rates as may be agreed upon from time to
time shall be charged.

The Special Defence Mileage Rates shall apply to :
Munitions of all kinds (including explosives).
Equipment and Ordnance Stores for Navy, Army, or Air.

consignment requiring, on account of its nature, special facilities or special trucks
for its conveyance.
