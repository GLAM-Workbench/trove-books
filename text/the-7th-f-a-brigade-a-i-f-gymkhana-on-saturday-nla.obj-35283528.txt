No, 4. SIGNALLERS' CONTEST.
Team to consist of 9 working N.C.O.’s or men and 11 N C.O.’s to supervise but
not take part.
Message to be correct in preference to shortest time taken in sending. Message
to be written down in correct manner on an “ A ” form.
Method of Sending Message Round,
Man to start from the Base equipped with Telephone, Earth pin and
Climbing irons.
Climb over obstacles as arranged by the Committee and, after clearing
the last obstacle, runs certain distance to a pole, from the top of which
a telegraph line has been run out. Climbs pole, connects up the terminals
and speaks a message through ’phone, Receiver at the other end writes
the message down and man with Morse flag sends it on,
Receiver at the Distant station receives message from the Hags and Writer-
down calls to man with the Disc, who sends the message on. Receiver at
Distant station calls to Telephonist, who writes down and Buzzes message
along wire, which is laid before race commences.

Receiver at Distant station writes message down correctly, disconnects his
telephone, packs up, mounts horse waiting for him, and after clearing
certain hurdles as arranged, he gallops back to base and hands message
back to Umpire.

No. 3. MAN-HANDLING GUN COMPETITION AND
CHANGING WHEELS,
CONDITIONS.
Man-handling Gun.
Position at starting point; Guns dressed and Detachment Rear. The Nos. I will
ne handed orders for coming into action and the Umpire will order “ Without
dragropes prepare to advance,” and he will immediately give the word “ Go ! ”
The detachment will advance at the double with the gun 50 yards to a line.
The first team past the line will receive 3 points, second 2 points and third
1 point.
As soon as the gun passes the line Nos. 1 will give instructions as contained in
orders. The first team to report “ Ready,” if correct, receives 3 points, second
2 points, third 1 point. Umpire will then order “ Cease firing and Limber up.”
The points as above will be allotted to the first, second and third teams in
forming Detachments Rear.
Changing Wheels.
This will be carried out as laid down in “ Standing Gun Drill.” The first team
to form Detachments Rear will receive points as above.
